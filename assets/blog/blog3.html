03 RoomController, Spawn Logic, and Role Assignment

Date: 9 Feb 2026 | Period: 9 Feb to 22 Feb 2026

Situation

The gameâ€™s actual multiplayer loop depends on a clean handoff from lobby to gameplay with the right player count, correct spawn positions, and balanced roles. This was the point where â€œnetworkingâ€ becomes â€œgame design.â€

Task

I needed to ensure each client spawns once, gets the right role, and lands in a spawn position that respects the role structure: one ghost, three priests.

Action

I made the master client responsible for orchestration and used actor number ordering plus delayed assignment checks so spawn and role RPCs do not race against object creation.

Result

Role assignment is now deterministic enough for actual gameplay tests. The lobby-to-match transition is no longer just a scene load; it produces a structured session.

Mechanics Delivered

  • Spawn mechanic with grounded fallback and role-aware placement.
  • Role distribution mechanic: 1 ghost, 3 priests.
  • Master-client authority mechanic for role assignment and start sequencing.
  • Scene cleanup mechanic to destroy invalid scene copies before spawn.
